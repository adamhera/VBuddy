V-Buddy

V-Buddy is a mentor-mentee concept exclusively only for Diploma in Computer Sciences, CS110 students at UiTM Kedah, where high-performing CS110 students may act as mentors for their peers. The program aims to facilitate personal growth for mentors and provide a comfortable learning environment for mentees by giving them access to their own mentor.

Mentor-mentee

Mentor-mentee is a relationship between an experienced person (mentor) and another person (mentee) who seeks guidance, advice, and support to develop their skills, knowledge, and abilities in a particular area. Mentor-mentee relationships rely on mutual respect and trust, with mentors guiding their mentees' personal and professional growth, while mentees take an active role in their development.

Mentor

In genereal, a mentor will:

Provide guidance, advice, and support to the mentee in a particular subject.

Help the mentee to develop their skills, knowledge, and abilities.

Provide feedback and constructive criticism to the mentee.

Help the mentee to build their confidence and self-esteem.

Mentee

In general, a mentee has the following responsibilities:

Be open to receiving guidance, advice, and support from the mentor.

Actively seek out opportunities to learn and grow.

Be receptive to feedback and constructive criticism.

Take responsibility for their own learning and development.

The outcomes for participating

Be a mentee and gain a deeper understanding of course material with the help of your mentors.

Be a mentor, share your knowledge and feel more connected to the academic community.

Together, improve academic performance among your coursemate.
